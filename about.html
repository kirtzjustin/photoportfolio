About Me

Bio

I'm a hobbyist photographer with a love for street and traveling. During the week I pay the bills by leading the GIS team at Greenville Water as the GIS Supervisor. Outside of work I enjoy spending time with my wife and daughter and playing entirely too much Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. Originally from Charlotte, North Carolina by way of Ohio, I have a B.S. in Geography with a concentration in GIS from Appalachian State University (2010).
